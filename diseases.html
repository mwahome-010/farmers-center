Blight

Causes

The disease is caused by a fungus called Alternaria alternata and is spread by the wind.

Blight is a fungal disease that affects the leaves of plants. It is caused by a fungus called Alternaria alternata.

Treatment Methods

Use a fungicide to treat the disease.

Prevention Methods

Use a fungicide to prevent the disease.

Symptoms

The leaves of the plant will turn brown and die.
